Leadership Practice
Inventory

A developmental assessment of leadership practice across five domains. Two tiers, one framework.

Basic Academy

LPI: Self-Assessment

Rate your own practice across 20 behavioral items and three open reflection questions. Results are reviewed by your GKG Leadership Advisor before your group session or Individual Feedback Conversation.

Advanced Academy

LPI: Multi-Rater

Gather behavioral observations from the people you lead, work alongside, and report to. Builds on your self-assessment with perspectives from multiple rater groups.

To access your survey

You should have received a personalized link from your GKG Leadership Advisor. Use that link to access your assessment. If you have not received a link, contact your GKG Leadership Advisor directly.

A note on the word leadership
Leadership, as we use the word here, is not a title or a position; it is a practice. It is the ongoing work of making decisions under uncertainty, enabling others to do their best work, and sustaining both yourself and your team through the demands of the mission. You do not have it or lack it. You do it, more or less effectively, depending on conditions, context, and the habits you have built over time.

Developmental purpose only

Time required
Approximately 20 to 25 minutes. Please complete in one sitting if possible.

Your results are private

Reflection Questions
Tell me more

Three questions to contextualize your ratings. Your responses help your GKG Leadership Advisor understand your practice in your specific context.

What makes reflection useful

The most useful responses describe a specific situation: what was happening, what you noticed about yourself, and what the impact was.

What was the situation? What did you do? What made it feel like your best work?

Can you be more specific? What was the situation, and what exactly did you do?
60 characters minimum

Can you name a specific pattern or situation where this comes up?
60 characters minimum
